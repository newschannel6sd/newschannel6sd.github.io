Date Written: 5/16/25

As summer approaches, San Diego is beginning to warm up. One of the results is that there is a lot of tourism and busy beaches. This might make finding a parking spot difficult. If you plan to stay on the beach, remember to wear sunscreen and watch out for stingrays. Also, make sure to set up near a lifeguard stand to have quick access to safety.
