San Diego Greets Year of the Snake with the 42nd Year Chinese New Year Fair. This event took place In downtown San Diego Saturday, February 8, and February 9, 10 AM to 5 PM at 3rd Avenue and J Street. The Year of the snake symbolizes wisdom, transformation, and determination. This event had many booths. This event had many different foods such as roasted chestnuts, bao buns, boba tea, and dumplings. This event is held to celebrate Chinese culture and bring a community together.
